Sustainability Advisory Committee

Good Health and Wellbeing

The sustainability Advisory Committee is committed to promoting and stimulating good health and well being in our community. We as a committee believe that a healthy and harmless environment is essential to the wellbeing of all individuals.

Our main iniatives are:

  • Promoting physical heath
  • End preventable deaths of newborns and children
  • Supporting mental health issues and awareness
  • Reduce premature mortality from non-communicable diseases
  • Improving air and water quality

Local Challenges

  • Healthcare accessibility in rural areas
  • Mental health service gaps
  • Aging population care needs
  • Health inequality in disadvantaged communities

Development Process

1.

Analyse and research

Analyse and research current health infrastructure and identify any outstanding gaps in the services which can be further developed and improved.

2.

Planning

Develop targeted interventions and implement new and effective strategies such as reseource allocation in which these new strategies can positively impact the community.

3.

Implementation and execution

Implement and Execute health programs and monitor progress which allows further improvements to be made within the programs if needed.

4.

Evaluation

Measure the outcomes and adjust aswell as enhmance strategies based on results.

Policy Recommendations

Healthcare Access

Implement mobile health units for rural areas and expand telemedicine services

Mental Health

Integrate mental health services into primary care and increase funding for community programs

Prevention

Strengthen early detection programs and health education initiatives
